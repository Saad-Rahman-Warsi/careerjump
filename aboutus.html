OUR STORY

In the year 2015, CareerJump was born to assist and empower aspiring job seekers in their interview preparation. Our humble beginnings saw us training around 20 individuals in the first month of our inception. Eight years later in the present year of 2023, we are excited to share that we now guide and support approximately 1000 job seekers each month, witnessing remarkable growth and success. We feel proud in having tremendous confidence of many. Not only this, we have also expanded our array of services and proudly offer mock interview, group discussions, aptitude tests and personality development sessions.

OUR MISSION

At CareerJump, our mission is to train individuals to give them skills, knowledge and confidence they need to excel in their career pursuits. We focus on four aspects realted to job search - interview, group discussions, aptitude test and personality development. Our learning sessions are well designed to significantly boost your chances of landing at a job. We offer a supportive learning environment giving individualized attention and expert advice to help one realize their true potential.
